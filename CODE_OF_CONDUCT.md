# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

FlitPDF is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and constructive community. We want contributors and users to feel safe participating regardless of experience level, background, identity, or skill set.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent, and discuss ideas without attacking people
- Offer constructive feedback with enough context to be useful
- Welcome questions from new contributors
- Respect project boundaries, maintainers' time, and community decisions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement
- Sharing private information without permission
- Misrepresenting your work, identity, or affiliation with the project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, releases, and other project spaces where people represent FlitPDF.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to `rohitbhure.cse@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, restrict participation, or take other appropriate action in response to behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Good Faith

We will review reports in good faith, aim to protect the affected people, and respond as fairly as possible based on the information available.
